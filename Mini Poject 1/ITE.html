The Department of Information Technology started in 2008 at Sandip Foundation's Sandip Institute of Technology and Research Centre, Nashik. The Department offers Undergraduate Program (UG) in Information Technology with an intake of 60. Since then, the department producing the IT engineer to meet the demands and the trends of the IT industries.The department has state-of-the-art laboratories, computing facilities and qualified faculty members. The Department has a student association named as "Student Association of Young Information Technology Professional" (SAYITp). It organizes various activities for the overall development of the student.
